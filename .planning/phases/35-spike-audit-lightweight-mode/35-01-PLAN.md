**Part A: Fix researcher Open Questions format**

In `agents/gsd-phase-researcher.md`, find the Open Questions section in the output_format template (around line 308-313). Replace the current flat list format:

```markdown

1. **[Question]**
   - What we know: [partial info]
   - What's unclear: [the gap]
   - Recommendation: [how to handle]
```

With the structured format that matches spike-integration.md expectations:

```markdown
## Open Questions

### Resolved
- {Question}: {How research answered it}

### Genuine Gaps
| Question | Criticality | Recommendation |
|----------|-------------|----------------|
| {Question} | Critical/Medium/Low | Spike/Defer/Accept-risk |

### Still Open
- {Questions that couldn't be resolved - flagged for attention}
```

This format has three subsections:
- "Resolved" -- questions research answered (most should be here)
- "Genuine Gaps" -- questions research could NOT answer, with the table format step 5.5 will parse
- "Still Open" -- questions that remain open but don't warrant spiking

Also update the structured_returns section's "Open Questions" line to reference this new format instead of just "[Gaps that couldn't be resolved]".
